About Us

Here at Techo Books, we're trying to keep the cost of technical books down and make old or specialized texts available. We don't maintain a physical showroom, which means our running cost are much lower compared to other dealers. This is a cost saving which we pass on to you, the buyer.
Instead maintaining a physical showroom, we have an online showroom. Here you can see full details of all our offerinngs with large and clear photos.
We have a number of steps to ensure that what you buy from us is value for money, including:

  • Our new books come direct from the publisher
  • Your order is tracked and mailed to you to ensure it will not be lost
  • 30-Day money-back guarantee on all orders
