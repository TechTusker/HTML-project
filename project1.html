 Welcome to TECHO BOOKS!

Techno books specializes in online sales of specialized technical publications and antique books.

In particular, we specialize in books in computing and mathematics.

Because we don't run a showroom, we are able to keep our prices down. All our books are in good conditions.

Our online showroom is divided into two parts-those for computing books, and those for mathematical texts. We think you will like our range.
